Ni, Chuan (Nick)

Teacher

I graduated in 2011, from Beijing Normal University, College of Life Science, as a PhD in Ecology. I have 6 years’ experiences of Biology teaching in SHSBNU international division. I have dedicated myself in creating different levels of Biology courses to fulfill demands of different students. I started form the Standard Level Biology (SLB), building Moodle version (online) of SLB and forwarded to Accelerated Biology Course (ABC) for enthusiastic students, as well as Honored Biology Course and Genetic Engineering Experiments for students who’s willing to study further and deeper in Biology. These different levels of Biology Courses prepared the students for international Biology contests, and are favorited by the students.
